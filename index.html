Model: RX-8

About:
The Mazda RX-8 is a compact sports car from the Japanese brand Mazda that was manufactured from 2003 to 2012. In its manual transmission version it is capable of generating 232 horsepower at 8250 rpm and 22.02 kgfm of torque at 5500 rpm in an engine of 1.3 L, which does 0–100 km/h 6.6 seconds according to Mazda. It uses the Wankel rotary engine. The RX-8 began North American sales in the year 2003.

RX 8 Specs, Features and Price

The Mazda RX 8 has 1 Petrol Engine on offer. The Petrol engine is 1308 cc . It is available with Manual transmission.Depending upon the variant and fuel type the RX 8 has a mileage of 19.0 kmpl . The RX 8 is a 4 seater 4 cylinder car.
